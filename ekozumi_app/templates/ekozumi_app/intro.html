EKOZUMI

How to play:

An anagram puzzle is released everyday, solve it to reveal a hint for that day's miniboss' location

Explore around campus while checking the map to find the miniboss' location. Once you are in the correct location a button will appear and you will be able to battle the boss

Pay attention to what they are saying, as at the end of the week you may fight a megaboss where your knowledge of the sustainability issues discussed will be tested

Have fun! Note: You must have location services enabled on your browser
